About

The Transfiguration of Christ Parish is relatively a new parish in the Diocese of Antipolo, having been canonically erected on December 12, 2002. The parish church is located along M.L. Quezon St. Ext., Brgy San Roque, Antipolo City.

Mission

Vision of the ToCP: "Isang Sambayanang Katoliko sa Diyosesis ng Antipolo na binubuo ng mga Munting Sambayanang Katoliko na hinuhubog sa pagbibiyaya at pagtawag ng Ebanghelyo -- sumasaksi sa pananampalataya, pagmamahalan, pagkakaisa, pagdadamayan at pagiging mabuting tagapangasiwa sa mga kaloob ng Diyos -- upang matugunan ang pangangailangan ng sangnilikha at makamit ang paghahari ng Diyos sa kanilang piling."

General Information

  • Parish Church dedicated on 12 December 2012.
  • Dedication Rites presided by Most Rev. Francisco de Leon, DD (Auxiliary Bishop of Antipolo) together with Most Rev. Roberto Mallari (Bishop of San Jose, Nueva Ecija) & Most Rev. Jose Rojas (Bishop of Libmanan, Camarines Sur).
  • Parish has its own Columbarium & Lazarus' Memorial Chapels.

Liturgical Services

  • Sunday Masses - 6:30 am / 8:00 am / 9:30 am / 5:00 pm / 6:30 pm
  • Saturday (Anticipated Sunday Mass) - 6:00 pm
  • Daily Mass (Monday to Saturday) - 6:15 am
  • Every Wednesday & First Friday - 6:00 pm
  • Baptism - Every Sunday at 11:30 am
  • Please inquire at the parish office for other services.
