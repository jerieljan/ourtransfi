About the Parish

The Transfiguration of Christ Parish is relatively a new parish in the Diocese of Antipolo, having been canonically erected on December 12, 2002. The parish church is located along M.L. Quezon St. Ext., Brgy San Roque, Antipolo City.

  • The parish Church was dedicated on December 12, 2012.
  • Dedication Rites were presided by Most Rev. Francisco de Leon, DD (Auxiliary Bishop of Antipolo) together with Most Rev. Roberto Mallari (Bishop of San Jose, Nueva Ecija) & Most Rev. Jose Rojas (Bishop of Libmanan, Camarines Sur).
  • The parish has its own Columbarium & Lazarus' Memorial Chapels.

Our Lady of Czestochowa

An icon received in June 17, 2014 from the National Shrine of Our Lady of Czestochowa in Jasna Gora, Poland. Our Lady of Czestochowa is enthroned in the sanctuary area of the Transfiguration of Christ Parish.

The parish first celebrated her feast day on August 26, 2014 (her feast day in Poland). She is now the special patroness of the Transfiguration of Christ Parish. It is hoped that devotion spread to Our Lady of Czestochowa and the parish may become a permanent shrine to her in the Diocese of Antipolo and in the whole Philippines.
